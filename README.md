## Case Study: Netflix Content Analytics
• Analyzed 5,283 Netflix Movies and TV shows (1953-2022) using SQL and Power BI, performing data cleaning, exploratory
data analysis, trend analysis, and KPI reporting to uncover audience engagement patterns & content performance across
70 years of entertainment data.
• Developed a popularity metric combining IMDB Ratings and Vote volume, enabling more reliable content evaluation and
identifying the highest-performing movies and TV shows in the dataset.
• Identified a strong temporal skew in the dataset, with 86.5% of movies released after 2010, improving analytical accuracy
and contextual interpretation of historical trends.
• Evaluated relationships between IMDb ratings,and age certifications through interactive Power BI dashboards, revealing
higher-rated TV shows and top-performing TV-14 and PG-13 content categories.
